Understanding Microfiber Technology

The Ultimate Cleaning & Detailing Material

Microfiber is a synthetic fabric composed of ultra-fine microscopic fibers—typically a blend of polyester and polyamide. These fibers are split to create millions of tiny hooks and loops, making microfiber vastly superior to traditional cotton in absorbing liquids and trapping dirt.

Superior Absorbency

Microfiber towels can absorb up to 7 times their weight in water, drying surfaces quickly and leaving zero streaks behind.

Exceptional Cleaning Power

The split-fiber structure acts like a magnet for dust, dirt, and grease, trapping particles deep within the fabric instead of pushing them around.

Lint-Free & Non-Abrasive

Incredibly soft and durable. Engineered to be safe on delicate surfaces like car paint, glass, and screens without scratching or shedding lint.
